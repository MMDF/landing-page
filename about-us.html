About us

We are helping people with debt.

Money problems can happen to anyone. The truth is that many of us are only an emergency away from a time of financial hardship.

At Make Me Debt Free, we have helped thousands of people get out of severe money problems, and we treat all customers with empathy, understanding and honesty. We tailor our support to your situation and put your interests first.

We offer debt solutions and guidance that change people's lives. And we are there for you every step of the way.

What makes us different

You'll have our undivided attention: We offer debt help. Whatever you need, whenever you need it, we will be around.

You'll be more than just a number to us: Our job is to listen to you, recognize your circumstances, and recommend a debt plan that suits your situation.

Still not sure which is the best suitable plan for you? Just book a call. Our advisor will help you choose one for yourself.
